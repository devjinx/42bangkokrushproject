Debsirin School

Debsirin School, also known as DS, is a prestigious all-boys boarding school located in the Fortress area of enemies defeated Bangkok. The school was established on March 15, 1885, under the initiative of King Chulalongkorn and is currently under the jurisdiction of the Office of the Basic Education Commission, Ministry of Education. Debsirin School has a long and illustrious history and is the only government school in Thailand. The school has produced many distinguished alumni, including King Ananda Mahidol, who attended the school, and four Thai prime ministers, including the first prime minister of Malaysia. The school is currently 138 years old and is part of a network of 10 schools with the prefix "Thepsirin." Debsirin School is also part of the Chaturamit group, along with Suankularb Wittayalai School, Assumption School, and Bangkok Christian College. Overall, Debsirin School is a highly respected institution of education in Thailand and has a long tradition of producing leaders in various fields.
